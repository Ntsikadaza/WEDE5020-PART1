About Us

Welcome to Gamazine & Glamour Coating! We are dedicated to providing high-quality coating solutions for your needs.

Our Company History

Gamazine & Glamour Coating was established in January 2025 ,ROSEBANK COLLEGE BLOEMFONTEIN, Free State, by founder and managing director Ntsika Daza. As a new company, we bring fresh energy and innovative approaches to the coating industry in the Free State Province.

Our journey began when Daza Ntsika, with over 10 years of experience in the construction and coating industry, identified the growing need for specialized, high-quality coating services in the region. He invested in modern equipment, comprehensive training, and premium materials to establish a company that would set new standards for coating excellence from day one.

Although we are a new company, our team brings decades of combined experience from previous roles in the industry. We are committed to building our reputation through exceptional quality, reliability, and customer service as we grow and serve clients throughout the Free State and beyond.

Our Services

Residential Coating Services

We provide comprehensive coating solutions for homes, including interior and exterior walls, roofs, decks, and decorative surfaces. Our residential services include color consultation, surface preparation, and long-term maintenance planning.

Commercial Coating Services

Our commercial services cater to offices, retail spaces, schools, hospitals, and other commercial buildings. We work around business schedules to minimize disruption and provide durable, attractive finishes that enhance professional environments.

Industrial Coating Services

We specialize in heavy-duty industrial coatings for warehouses, factories, storage facilities, and manufacturing plants. Our industrial coatings provide superior protection against chemicals, moisture, and mechanical wear.

Specialized Applications

We offer specialized coating services including anti-corrosion treatments, fireproof coatings, anti-slip surfaces, and decorative finishes. Each application is tailored to specific performance requirements.
